About Vachanti
Photography School:

We are a photography school with more than 20 years of experience, we are specialized in training dedicated and talented photographers. Our academy has a long history in the implementation of training in technical and aesthetic skills, in addition to having the necessary experience and infrastructure to provide you with a solid photographic training, in a warm and pleasant environment, providing the necessary space for the creativity and artistic production. Join us to open and expand your path as a professional photographer.

Values that represent us:

  • Respect
  • Commitment
  • Humility
  • Solidarity
  • Honesty
  • Responsability

What is our goal?

Our goal will always be to offer a broad accessible and guaranteed education to all those people who want to enter the world of photography. And promote the student to the search of his own vocation through various courses and workshops to learn the management of the camera, the focus and the composition of creative images.

Why study photography?

Photography will help you open your eyes to what surrounds you. You will be attentive to details to capture it and therefore you will have a completely new perception of your surroundings. This wonderful career provides tools and techniques to make art through images, to convey ideas with photos or to give them an approach with which facts can be narrated. So, what are you waiting for to start?
